CLIMATE PATTERNS: EL NINO IMPACT ON REGIONAL FARMING
Meteorological Center Report, 2026.
The emerging El Niño Southern Oscillation (ENSO) pattern is projected to shift typical seasonal rainfall windows.
Expected Impacts:
- Higher than average summer temperatures.
- Prolonged dry periods during early vegetative growth stages of corn crops.
- Severe thunderstorm pockets causing soil compaction and nutrient leaching in low-lying fields.
Mitigation:
- Ensure irrigation equipment is serviced and operational by early June.
- Implement conservation tillage to retain maximum soil moisture.
